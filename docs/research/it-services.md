## Above-Fold Patterns

### Hero Section Structure

1. **Visual Elements**
   - City skyline (for local MSPs)
   - Professional team imagery
   - Technology/data center visuals
   - Client success imagery
   - Abstract tech graphics (subtle)
   - Dashboard/monitoring screenshots

2. **Headline Formulas**
   - Local positioning: "[City]'s IT Services Provider"
   - Outcome-focused: "IT That Works So You Can Focus on Business"
   - Problem-solution: "Stop Fighting Technology. Start Growing."
   - Trust/experience: "25+ Years of IT Excellence"
   - Specific benefit: "Reduce IT Issues by 90%"
   - Question: "Is Your IT Holding Your Business Back?"

3. **Pre-headline Elements** (Important for MSPs)
   - "[City]'s Trusted IT Partner"
   - "Managed IT for [Industry]"
   - "Enterprise-Grade IT for Growing Businesses"

4. **Above-fold Must-Haves**
   - Phone number (sales + support separate)
   - Primary CTA ("Get IT Assessment")
   - Proof points (years, clients, devices managed)
   - Trust indicators (certifications, reviews)
   - Industry/size indicators

### Visual Design Patterns
- Professional, corporate aesthetic
- Blue color schemes dominant (trust)
- Clean, uncluttered layouts
- Partner/certification logos
- Statistics/numbers prominent
- Local imagery (city backgrounds)

## CTA Patterns

### Primary CTAs

| CTA Text | Purpose |
|----------|---------|
| "Get Free IT Assessment" | Lead capture |
| "Request a Quote" | Sales inquiry |
| "Schedule a Call" | Consultation booking |
| "Calculate Your IT Costs" | Interactive tool |
| "Download Our Guide" | Lead magnet |

### B2B MSP CTA Hierarchy
1. **Assessment/Consultation** - Primary
2. **Call to Discuss** - Secondary
3. **Resource Download** - Lead nurture
4. **Pricing Calculator** - Self-service

### CTA Placement Strategy
- Hero section: Primary assessment CTA
- After services: Service-specific CTAs
- Testimonials: "Join Our Clients"
- Pricing area: "Get Custom Quote"
- Sticky header: Phone + "Contact Us"

## Section Structure (Full Page)

### Recommended Section Order

1. **Header**
   - Logo
   - Main navigation
   - Phone numbers (Sales | Support)
   - Primary CTA button

2. **Hero Section**
   - Pre-headline (local/industry positioning)
   - Main headline (1-2 lines max)
   - Sub-headline or bullet benefits
   - Primary CTA
   - Trust indicators/proof points

3. **Trust Bar**
   - Key statistics
   - Years | Clients | Devices | Satisfaction

4. **Services Overview**
   - Managed IT Support
   - Cybersecurity
   - Cloud Services
   - Help Desk
   - Network Management
   - Backup & Recovery
   - VoIP/Communications
   - IT Consulting

5. **Why Choose Us**
   - Proactive vs reactive approach
   - Fixed monthly pricing
   - Local support team
   - Security-first mindset
   - Scalable solutions
   - Industry expertise

6. **Industries Served**
   - Healthcare
   - Finance
   - Legal
   - Manufacturing
   - Non-Profit
   - Professional Services

7. **How We Work / Process**
   - Step 1: Discovery Call
   - Step 2: IT Assessment
   - Step 3: Custom Proposal
   - Step 4: Onboarding
   - Step 5: Ongoing Support

8. **Pricing Calculator/Info**
   - Interactive calculator (if available)
   - Pricing factors explanation
   - "No hidden fees" messaging
   - CTA to get custom quote

9. **Case Studies / Results**
   - Client success stories
   - Before/after metrics
   - Industry-specific examples

10. **Testimonials**
    - Client quotes
    - Company names (if permitted)
    - Video testimonials

11. **Certifications & Partners**
    - Partner logos
    - Certification badges
    - Award recognition

12. **Resources Section**
    - Blog previews
    - Downloadable guides
    - Webinar recordings

13. **FAQ Section**
    - Pricing questions
    - Service questions
    - Onboarding questions
    - Support questions

14. **Final CTA**
    - "Ready to Transform Your IT?"
    - Assessment request form
    - Phone number

15. **Footer**
    - Contact info
    - Service areas
    - Quick links
    - Certifications
    - Privacy/legal

## Objection Handling

### Common Objections & Responses

| Objection | Landing Page Solution |
|-----------|----------------------|
| "MSPs are too expensive" | ROI calculator, cost comparison, "predict IT costs" |
| "We have internal IT" | Co-managed IT options, augmentation positioning |
| "I'm locked into a contract" | "We help with transitions", contract analysis offer |
| "How fast will you respond?" | SLA guarantees, response time stats |
| "Will you understand our industry?" | Industry-specific pages, compliance expertise |
| "What if we grow/change?" | Scalability messaging, flexible contracts |
| "How do I know you're reliable?" | Uptime guarantees, redundancy explanation |
| "Data security concerns" | Security certifications, protocols, SOC 2 |

### Objection-Handling Content Blocks

**Cost Objection Handler**
```
"Predictable IT Costs That Make Sense"

The Break-Fix Problem:
- Unpredictable costs
- Downtime = lost revenue
- Reactive, not proactive

The Managed IT Solution:
- Fixed monthly fee
- Avg savings: 25-40% vs in-house
- Prevention costs less than repair
- [Calculate Your Potential Savings]
```

**Control Objection Handler**
```
"Your IT, Our Partnership"
We don't replace your IT—we enhance it.

Co-Managed IT Options:
- You stay in control
- We handle the heavy lifting
- 24/7 backup support
- Specialized expertise on-demand
```

**Trust/Security Handler**
```
"Enterprise-Grade Security for Your Business"
- SOC 2 Type II Certified
- 24/7 Security Monitoring
- Employee Background Checks
- Encrypted Communications
- Regular Security Audits
- [See Our Security Practices]
```

### Key Conversion Metrics
- Assessment form conversion: 2-5% (B2B typical)
- Phone call rate: 3-5%
- Calculator engagement: 15-25%
- Lead-to-meeting: 30-40%
- Meeting-to-proposal: 50-60%

### A/B Testing Priorities
1. Headline (local vs outcome-focused)
2. Primary CTA text (Assessment vs Consultation)
3. Form length (5 vs 8 fields)
4. Proof points placement
5. Calculator vs standard form
6. Industry-specific vs general approach
