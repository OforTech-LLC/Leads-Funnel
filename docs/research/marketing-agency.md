## Above-Fold Patterns

### Hero Section Structure

1. **Visual Elements**
   - Results/metrics visualization
   - Client logo showcase
   - Team/people imagery
   - Abstract design elements
   - Dashboard/analytics screenshots
   - Video backgrounds (agency story)

2. **Headline Formulas**
   - Results-focused: "10X Your Marketing ROI"
   - Problem-solution: "Stop Wasting Ad Spend"
   - Credibility: "Trusted by 500+ Growing Brands"
   - Question: "Ready to Dominate Your Market?"
   - Specific outcome: "Double Your Leads in 90 Days"
   - Niche: "The E-commerce Marketing Experts"

3. **Sub-headline Elements**
   - Specific result promise
   - Client type indicator
   - Service highlight
   - Free offer mention
   - Guarantee statement

4. **Above-fold Must-Haves**
   - Primary CTA ("Get Free Strategy Session")
   - Client logos (social proof)
   - Results metrics
   - Trust badges (awards, reviews)
   - Video testimonial or case study teaser

### Visual Design Patterns

- Bold, modern aesthetics
- Brand-forward design (shows capabilities)
- Data visualization elements
- High-quality client work samples
- Professional team imagery
- Clean, conversion-focused layouts

## Section Structure (Full Page)

### Recommended Section Order

1. **Hero Section**
   - Bold headline with result promise
   - Sub-headline with specificity
   - Primary CTA
   - Client logos
   - Key metric or proof point

2. **Results Bar**
   - Key statistics
   - Revenue generated
   - Leads delivered
   - Client retention

3. **Services Overview**
   - SEO
   - PPC/Paid Media
   - Social Media Marketing
   - Content Marketing
   - Email Marketing
   - Web Design/CRO
   - Each with brief description and CTA

4. **Case Studies/Results**
   - 3-4 featured case studies
   - Industry variety
   - Specific metrics
   - "View All Case Studies" link

5. **Why Choose Us**
   - Data-driven approach
   - Transparent reporting
   - Dedicated team
   - Proven results
   - Industry expertise
   - Custom strategies

6. **Our Process**
   - Step 1: Discovery
   - Step 2: Strategy
   - Step 3: Execute
   - Step 4: Optimize
   - Step 5: Report & Scale

7. **Industries Served** (if relevant)
   - E-commerce
   - SaaS
   - Healthcare
   - Finance
   - Professional Services

8. **ROI Calculator** (Interactive)
   - Input current metrics
   - Show potential improvement
   - Capture lead info

9. **Testimonials**
   - Video testimonials (preferred)
   - Written testimonials with photos
   - Star ratings
   - Results-focused quotes

10. **Team/About Section**
    - Key team members
    - Expertise highlights
    - Culture glimpse

11. **Awards & Recognition**
    - Industry awards
    - Partner badges
    - Media mentions

12. **FAQ Section**
    - Pricing questions
    - Process questions
    - Results timeline
    - Contract questions

13. **Final CTA**
    - Strong closing headline
    - Consultation form
    - Phone number

14. **Footer**
    - Contact info
    - Service links
    - Resource links
    - Social media
    - Legal

## Objection Handling

### Common Objections & Responses

| Objection                            | Landing Page Solution                                |
| ------------------------------------ | ---------------------------------------------------- |
| "Agencies are too expensive"         | ROI focus, "investment not cost", case study ROI     |
| "I've been burned before"            | Transparent reporting, no long contracts, guarantees |
| "Can you handle my industry?"        | Industry-specific case studies, niche expertise      |
| "How long until I see results?"      | Timeline expectations, quick wins showcase           |
| "We tried marketing, it didn't work" | Audit offer, "find out what went wrong"              |
| "I don't have a big budget"          | Tiered services, starting packages, ROI calculator   |
| "How do I know you're different?"    | Unique methodology, awards, specific results         |
| "What about communication?"          | Dedicated account manager, reporting schedule        |

### Objection-Handling Content Blocks

**Cost/Value Objection Handler**

```
"Marketing That Pays for Itself"

Our average client sees:
- 3X return on ad spend
- 200% increase in qualified leads
- 40% reduction in customer acquisition cost

That's not an expense—it's an investment.
[See the Numbers →]
```

**Trust/Risk Objection Handler**

```
"We're Not Like Other Agencies"

✓ No long-term contracts
✓ Transparent, real-time reporting
✓ Dedicated team (not a rotation)
✓ Results guarantee*
✓ Weekly strategy calls

*We're so confident, if we don't hit goals,
we work free until we do.
```

**Results Timeline Handler**

```
"When Will I See Results?"

Month 1: Audit + Strategy + Setup
Month 2-3: Launch + Initial Data
Month 3-6: Optimization + Growth
Month 6+: Scale + Expand

Quick wins? Many clients see
improvement within 30 days.
```

### Key Conversion Metrics

- Strategy call conversion: 3-8%
- ROI calculator engagement: 15-25%
- Case study click-through: 10-15%
- Lead-to-call: 30-40%
- Call-to-proposal: 50-60%

### A/B Testing Priorities

1. Headline (result promise vs problem-focused)
2. Social proof placement (logos vs testimonials)
3. Form length (5 vs 8 fields)
4. Video testimonial presence
5. Guarantee messaging
6. CTA text variations
