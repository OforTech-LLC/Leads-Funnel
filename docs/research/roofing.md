## Above-Fold Patterns

- **Headline**: Problem-solution focused ("Leaking Roof? Get It Fixed Today")
- **Hero Visual**: Before/after photos, workers on roof, completed projects
- **Phone Number**: Click-to-call prominently displayed (emergency services)
- **Form**: Short form (name, phone, address) above fold
- **Urgency**: "Same Day Estimates", "24/7 Emergency Service"
- **Local**: City/area name in headline

## CTA Patterns

| Type       | Examples                                             |
| ---------- | ---------------------------------------------------- |
| Estimate   | "Get a Free Estimate", "Free Roof Inspection"        |
| Emergency  | "Call Now for Emergency Repair", "24/7 Service"      |
| Discount   | "$100 Off Your Roof Repair", "Spring Special"        |
| Assessment | "Schedule Your Free Assessment", "Roof Health Check" |

## Form Field Patterns

**Minimal (Highest Conversion)**

- Phone number only
- Zip code only

**Standard**

- Name
- Phone
- Email
- Address/Zip
- Service needed (dropdown)

**Multi-Step (Higher Quality Leads)**

- Step 1: Service type (Repair/Replace/Inspection)
- Step 2: Roof type (Shingle/Tile/Metal)
- Step 3: Timeline (ASAP/This Month/Just Exploring)
- Step 4: Contact info

## Trust Signals

- Years in business ("Serving [City] Since 1995")
- Number of roofs completed
- Manufacturer certifications (GAF, Owens Corning)
- Insurance and licensing badges
- BBB accreditation
- Google reviews with star rating
- Before/after photo galleries
- Warranty information
- "Locally Owned & Operated"

## Section Structure

1. **Hero** - Problem headline + phone + form
2. **Services** - Repair, replacement, inspection cards
3. **Why Choose Us** - 4-6 differentiators with icons
4. **Before/After Gallery** - Visual proof of work
5. **Process** - How we work (3-4 steps)
6. **Testimonials** - Video and written reviews
7. **Service Areas** - Map or city list
8. **Certifications** - Manufacturer badges
9. **FAQ** - Common roofing questions
10. **Final CTA** - Phone + form repeat

## Objection Handling

| Objection                 | How They Address It                             |
| ------------------------- | ----------------------------------------------- |
| "Roofers are scammers"    | Licensed, insured, BBB accredited, real reviews |
| "Too expensive"           | Free estimates, financing options, price match  |
| "Don't know if I need it" | Free inspection, honest assessment              |
| "Bad past experience"     | Satisfaction guarantee, warranty                |
| "Will take too long"      | Same-day estimates, timeline guarantees         |

## Conversion Blueprint

### Recommended Headline Formula

"[City]'s #1 [Service Type] — [Guarantee/Offer]" Example: "Miami's #1 Roof Repair Experts — Free
Inspection + $100 Off"

### Section Order

1. Hero with phone number and short form
2. Services Grid (3-4 main services)
3. Trust Bar (certifications, years, reviews count)
4. Before/After Gallery (slider)
5. Why Choose Us (4 icons with brief text)
6. Process Steps (3-4 numbered steps)
7. Testimonials (mix of video and text)
8. Financing Options (if applicable)
9. Service Area Map
10. FAQ Accordion
11. Final CTA with urgency

### Form Fields

- Name (required)
- Phone (required)
- Email (optional)
- Address or Zip (required)
- Service Type (dropdown: Repair, Replace, Inspection, Storm Damage)
- Best time to call (optional)

### CTA Wording

- Primary: "Get Your Free Roof Inspection"
- Phone: "Call Now: (305) XXX-XXXX"
- Secondary: "Schedule an Estimate"
- Urgency: "Same Day Appointments Available"
