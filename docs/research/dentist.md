## Above-Fold Patterns

- **Headline**: Benefit-focused ("Get the Smile You Deserve")
- **Hero Visual**: Smiling patient, modern office, dentist with patient
- **Special Offer**: "New Patient Special: $99 Cleaning + Exam"
- **Form**: Appointment request form
- **Phone**: Click-to-call for immediate booking
- **Trust**: "As Seen In" media logos, 5-star rating

## CTA Patterns

| Type | Examples |
|------|----------|
| Appointment | "Book Your Appointment", "Schedule Now" |
| Special | "Claim Your New Patient Offer", "Get $100 Off" |
| Assessment | "Free Smile Assessment", "Consultation Request" |
| Emergency | "Emergency? Call Now", "Same Day Appointments" |

## Form Field Patterns

**Minimal (New Patient)**
- Name
- Phone
- Email
- Preferred date/time

**Standard**
- Full name
- Phone
- Email
- Insurance (dropdown or checkbox)
- Service interested in
- Preferred appointment time

**Extended**
- Date of birth
- How did you hear about us?
- Current dental concerns
- Insurance provider name

## Trust Signals

- Patient testimonials with before/after photos
- Video testimonials
- Google/Yelp star ratings
- "As seen in" media mentions
- Dentist credentials and certifications
- Years of experience
- Number of happy patients
- Association memberships (ADA, local dental society)
- Modern technology mentions (digital X-rays, etc.)
- Payment/financing options

## Section Structure

1. **Hero** - Smile headline + offer + booking form
2. **New Patient Special** - Clear offer details
3. **Services** - Service cards (General, Cosmetic, Orthodontics)
4. **Meet the Doctor** - Photo, credentials, personal touch
5. **Before/After Gallery** - Treatment results
6. **Patient Testimonials** - Video preferred
7. **Technology** - Modern equipment, comfort features
8. **Insurance/Financing** - Payment flexibility
9. **Location/Hours** - Map, contact info
10. **FAQ** - Common patient questions
11. **Final CTA** - Repeat booking offer

## Objection Handling

| Objection | How They Address It |
|-----------|---------------------|
| "Dental work is painful" | Sedation options, comfort-focused language |
| "Too expensive" | Financing, insurance accepted, new patient specials |
| "Scared of dentist" | "Gentle care", "anxiety-free", calming office photos |
| "Don't have time" | Same-day appointments, extended hours, quick visits |
| "Bad past experience" | Patient-centered care, testimonials about comfort |

## Conversion Blueprint

### Recommended Headline Formula
"[Desired Outcome] in [Location] — [Special Offer]"
Example: "Get Your Perfect Smile in Miami — New Patients: $99 Cleaning & Exam"

### Section Order
1. Hero with appointment form and phone
2. New Patient Special (prominently displayed)
3. Services Overview (3-4 main categories)
4. Meet the Doctor(s) with credentials
5. Before/After Gallery
6. Patient Testimonials (video + written)
7. Office Tour/Technology
8. Insurance & Payment Options
9. Location with map
10. FAQ Accordion
11. Final CTA repeat

### Form Fields
- Name (required)
- Phone (required)
- Email (required)
- Service interested in (dropdown)
- Preferred date (date picker)
- Preferred time (morning/afternoon/evening)
- Insurance? (yes/no checkbox)

### CTA Wording
- Primary: "Book Your Appointment"
- Special: "Claim Your New Patient Offer"
- Soft: "Request a Free Consultation"
- Emergency: "Dental Emergency? Call Now"
