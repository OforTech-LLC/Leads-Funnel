## Above-Fold Patterns

### Hero Section Structure

1. **Visual Elements**
   - Clean, sparkling office environment
   - Professional cleaning crew in uniform
   - Before/after office spaces
   - Green cleaning products (if eco-focused)
   - Specific facility type imagery (office, medical, industrial)

2. **Headline Formulas**
   - Problem-focused: "A Clean Office Starts Here"
   - B2B Value: "Professional Cleaning Your Business Can Count On"
   - Local SEO: "Tampa's #1 Commercial Cleaning Service"
   - Specificity: "Office Cleaning Services for Businesses of All Sizes"
   - Trust: "Reliable Janitorial Services Since [Year]"
   - Results: "Cleaner Facilities. Happier Employees."

3. **Sub-headline Elements**
   - Industry specialization
   - Free quote offer
   - No-contract flexibility
   - Satisfaction guarantee
   - Certifications (green cleaning, etc.)

4. **Above-fold Must-Haves**
   - "Get Free Quote" CTA (primary)
   - Phone number
   - Industry served list
   - Trust indicators (BBB, certifications)
   - Service area mention

### Visual Design Patterns
- Clean, professional aesthetic (literally)
- Blue/green color schemes (trust + clean)
- High-quality facility imagery
- Uniformed staff photos
- Before/after comparisons
- Eco-friendly visual cues (if applicable)

## CTA Patterns

| CTA Text | Purpose |
|----------|---------|

| "Request a Facility Consultation" | Premium positioning |
| "Schedule My Cleaning" | Booking focus |

1. **Free Quote/Consultation** - Primary
2. **Call to Discuss** - Secondary

### CTA Placement Strategy
- Hero section: Primary quote CTA

- Testimonials section: "Join Our Clients"
- Pricing area: "Get Custom Quote"

## Form Field Patterns

### Quick Quote Form (B2B Optimized)
```
- Company Name *
- Contact Name *
- Phone Number *
- Email *
- Facility Type (dropdown)
- Square Footage (ranges)
```

### Detailed Commercial Quote Form
```
- Company Name *
- Contact Person *
- Title/Role
- Phone Number *
- Email *
- Facility Address
- Facility Type:
  □ Office Building
  □ Medical/Healthcare
  □ Retail
  □ Industrial/Warehouse
  □ Educational
  □ Restaurant/Food Service
  □ Fitness Center
  □ Other
- Approximate Square Footage:
  ○ Under 5,000 sq ft
  ○ 5,000 - 15,000 sq ft
  ○ 15,000 - 50,000 sq ft
  ○ 50,000+ sq ft
- Services Needed:
  □ Daily Janitorial
  □ Weekly Deep Clean
  □ Floor Care
  □ Window Cleaning
  □ Carpet Cleaning
  □ Restroom Sanitation
  □ Disinfection Services
  □ Post-Construction
- Cleaning Frequency:
  ○ Daily
  ○ Multiple times per week
  ○ Weekly
  ○ Bi-weekly
  ○ Monthly
  ○ One-time
- Current Provider? (Yes/No)
- Budget Range (optional)
- Best Time to Contact
- How did you hear about us?
```

### Form Best Practices
- Company name first (B2B signal)
- Square footage ranges (not exact)
- Multi-select for services
- Frequency selector important
- Budget optional but helpful
- Mobile-friendly dropdowns

## Section Structure (Full Page)

### Recommended Section Order

1. **Hero Section**
   - Headline + subheadline
   - Quick quote form or CTA
   - Trust badges
   - Client logos bar

2. **Services Overview**
   - Service categories with icons
   - Expandable descriptions
   - "Learn More" links

3. **Industries Served**
   - Office/Corporate
   - Healthcare/Medical
   - Retail
   - Education
   - Industrial
   - Hospitality
   - Each with specific benefits

4. **Why Choose Us**
   - Trained & vetted staff
   - Customized plans
   - Quality assurance
   - Green cleaning options
   - 24/7 availability

5. **Our Process**
   - Step 1: Free Consultation
   - Step 2: Custom Plan
   - Step 3: Service Begins
   - Step 4: Quality Checks

6. **Cleaning Checklist Preview**
   - What's included
   - Customization options
   - Frequency options

7. **Case Studies**
   - Before/after results
   - Client challenges solved
   - Measurable outcomes

8. **Testimonials**
   - Facility manager quotes
   - Company names (if permitted)
   - Video testimonials

9. **Green Cleaning Section** (if applicable)
   - Eco-friendly products
   - Sustainability practices
   - Certifications

10. **Service Area**
    - Map
    - Cities/regions served
    - Multiple location info

11. **FAQ Section**
    - Pricing questions
    - Contract terms
    - Insurance/bonding
    - Scheduling

12. **Final CTA**
    - "Request Your Free Facility Consultation"
    - Phone + form

13. **Footer**
    - Contact details
    - Service areas
    - Certifications
    - Quick links

## Objection Handling

### Common Objections & Responses

| Objection | Landing Page Solution |
|-----------|----------------------|
| "How much does it cost?" | "Custom quotes based on your needs", pricing factors explanation |
| "Are your workers trustworthy?" | Background check policy, bonding/insurance details |
| "What if I'm not satisfied?" | Satisfaction guarantee, re-clean policy |
| "Do you use safe products?" | Green cleaning certifications, product info |
| "Will you damage our property?" | Insurance coverage, liability limits |
| "Can you handle our industry?" | Industry-specific pages, relevant certifications |
| "What about security?" | Key/alarm protocols, NDA availability |
| "Are you flexible with scheduling?" | 24/7 availability, custom scheduling |

### Objection-Handling Content Blocks

**Cost Objection Handler**
```
"How Much Does Commercial Cleaning Cost?"
Pricing depends on:
- Square footage

- Special requirements

- Secure key management protocols

```

**Quality Assurance Handler**
```
"100% Satisfaction Guaranteed"
- Regular quality inspections
- Direct communication with your account manager
- 24-hour response to concerns
- Re-clean if not satisfied
```

3. Client logo placement
4. Industry-specific vs general approach

## Mobile-Specific Patterns

### Critical Mobile Elements
- Click-to-call prominent
- Simplified quote form (5 fields max)
- Collapsible service sections
- Easy industry selection
- Swipeable testimonials
- Fast loading (compressed images)

### Mobile Form Optimization
- Dropdown menus over typing
- Square footage ranges (not exact)
- Checkbox multi-select
- Progress indicator for multi-step
- Auto-save form progress

- "Industry-Specific Cleaning Standards"

### Follow-up Strategy
- Immediate email confirmation
- Same-day phone call

- Custom proposal timeline
