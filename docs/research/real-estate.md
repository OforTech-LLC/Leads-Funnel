# Real Estate Landing Page Research

## Reference Landing Pages

1. [Redfin](https://www.redfin.com/) - "Sell for top dollar for HALF the listing fee"
2. [Sundae](https://sundae.com/) - Focus on selling quickly at higher prices
3. [StreetEasy](https://streeteasy.com/) - Minimalist design with clear segmentation
4. [Corcoran](https://www.corcoran.com/) - Empathy-driven seller landing page
5. [Flyhomes](https://www.flyhomes.com/) - "Buy before you sell" concept
6. [Zillow](https://www.zillow.com/) - Home valuation lead capture
7. [Opendoor](https://www.opendoor.com/) - Instant offer positioning
8. [Compass](https://www.compass.com/) - Luxury market focus
9. [Realtor.com](https://www.realtor.com/) - Property search focused
10. [Luxury Presence](https://www.luxurypresence.com/) - Agent-focused luxury branding

## Above-Fold Patterns

- **Headline**: Clear value proposition (save money, sell faster, find dream home)
- **Hero Visual**: High-quality property images or lifestyle shots
- **Search/Form**: Address input or property search prominently placed
- **Trust Badge**: "Rated #1", "1M+ homes sold", agent credentials
- **CTA**: Single clear action (Get estimate, Search homes, Contact agent)

## CTA Patterns

| Type | Examples |
|------|----------|
| Valuation | "Get Your Free Home Value", "What's My Home Worth?" |
| Search | "Search Homes", "Find Your Dream Home" |
| Contact | "Schedule a Consultation", "Talk to an Agent" |
| Offer | "Get Your Cash Offer", "See Your Offer" |

## Form Field Patterns

**Minimal (Highest Conversion)**
- Property address only
- Email only

**Standard**
- Full name
- Email
- Phone
- Property address (for sellers)
- Budget range (for buyers)

**Extended**
- Timeline (When looking to buy/sell?)
- Pre-approval status
- Property type preference

## Trust Signals

- Number of transactions/homes sold
- Customer testimonials with photos
- Media mentions (NY Magazine, WSJ)
- Real estate association badges (NAR, MLS)
- Agent headshot and credentials
- Google/Zillow rating stars
- "As seen in" press logos

## Section Structure

1. **Hero** - Value prop + primary CTA + search/form
2. **How It Works** - 3-step process visualization
3. **Benefits** - Why choose us (3-4 key differentiators)
4. **Social Proof** - Testimonials with specific results
5. **Featured Listings/Services** - Cards showcasing offerings
6. **Local Expertise** - Market knowledge, neighborhood focus
7. **FAQ** - Common questions addressed
8. **Final CTA** - Repeat primary action

## Objection Handling

| Objection | How They Address It |
|-----------|---------------------|
| "Agents cost too much" | Highlight savings (1% listing fee vs 3%) |
| "Process is stressful" | Emphasize concierge service, hand-holding |
| "Don't know market value" | Free instant home valuation |
| "Not ready yet" | "Just exploring? That's okay" soft CTA |
| "Don't trust online" | Local agent photos, credentials, reviews |

## Conversion Blueprint

### Recommended Headline Formula
"[Benefit] Your [Property Type] in [Location] — [Unique Value Prop]"
Example: "Sell Your Miami Home for 20% More — In Half the Time"

### Section Order
1. Hero with address input or consultation CTA
2. 3-Step Process (Simple visualization)
3. Key Benefits (3 cards: Save Money, Sell Faster, Expert Guidance)
4. Market Stats/Results (Numbers that prove value)
5. Testimonials (Video preferred, 2-3 written)
6. Service Areas (Map or neighborhood cards)
7. FAQ Accordion (5-7 questions)
8. Final CTA Section (Repeat hero offer)

### Form Fields
- Name (required)
- Email (required)
- Phone (required)
- Property Address (required for sellers)
- "Best time to contact" (optional dropdown)

### CTA Wording
- Primary: "Get Your Free Home Valuation"
- Secondary: "Schedule a No-Obligation Consultation"
- Soft: "See Homes in My Area"
