## Above-Fold Patterns

### Headlines

- **Problem-Solution Format**: "AC Not Cooling? Get Same-Day Service"
- **Benefit-Focused**: "Stay Comfortable Year-Round with Expert HVAC Service"
- **Localized**: "Affordable Heating Repair for [City] Homes"
- **Urgency-Based**: "24/7 Emergency HVAC Services Available Now"

### Subheadlines

- Emphasize comfort, energy savings, or improved air quality
- Include service area mention
- Highlight availability ("Licensed technicians available 24/7")

### Hero Images

- Professional technician working on HVAC equipment
- Comfortable family at home
- Branded service vehicle
- Modern HVAC unit installation

### Value Props (Above Fold)

- 24/7 emergency service availability
- Licensed and insured technicians
- Free estimates or diagnostics
- Same-day service guarantee
- Years of experience in the area

## CTA Patterns

### Primary CTA Text

- "Get My Free Estimate"
- "Schedule Service Now"
- "Book Appointment Today"
- "Call Now for Same-Day Service"
- "Request Your Quote"

### CTA Placement

1. **Above fold** - Primary CTA button next to or below headline
2. **Sticky header/footer** - Mobile-optimized, follows user while scrolling
3. **After each section** - Repeated throughout page
4. **Bottom of page** - Final conversion opportunity

### Urgency Triggers

- "Limited Slots Available Today"
- "Book Before 5 PM for Same-Day Service"
- "Emergency? We're Available 24/7"
- Countdown timers for seasonal offers

### CTA Button Design

- High contrast color (typically orange, red, or green on blue/white backgrounds)
- Large, easily tappable on mobile (minimum 44px height)
- Action-oriented verb starting the text
- Icon paired with text (phone icon, calendar icon)

## Form Field Patterns

### Minimal Form (Highest Conversion)

1. Name
2. Phone Number
3. Email (optional)
4. Service Type (dropdown)

### Standard Form

1. Name
2. Phone Number
3. Email
4. Service Type (dropdown: AC Repair, Heating Repair, Installation, Maintenance, Emergency)
5. Preferred Contact Time

### Extended Form (Lower Conversion, Higher Quality Leads)

1. Name
2. Phone Number
3. Email
4. Address/Zip Code
5. Service Type
6. Brief Problem Description
7. Preferred Contact Time

### Form Best Practices

- Limit to 3-4 fields for maximum conversion (25% vs 15% with 7+ fields)
- Use clear labels and placeholder text
- Include form validation with instant feedback
- Action-oriented submit button ("Get My Free Estimate" not "Submit")
- Add privacy assurance text below form

## Trust Signals

### Certifications & Licenses

- NATE Certified Technicians
- EPA Certified
- State Contractor License Number displayed
- BBB Accredited Business
- ACCA Member

### Guarantees

- 100% Satisfaction Guarantee
- Price Match Guarantee
- Workmanship Warranty (1-5 years)
- "No Surprise Pricing" Promise
- Money-Back Guarantee

### Social Proof

- Google Reviews rating with star count
- Number of reviews ("500+ 5-Star Reviews")
- Customer testimonials with names and photos
- "Trusted by X,XXX Homeowners in [City]"
- Video testimonials

### Industry Partnerships

- Carrier, Trane, Lennox authorized dealer badges
- EnergyStar partner
- Manufacturer warranty badges

### Insurance & Bonding

- "Fully Licensed & Insured"
- Workers compensation coverage mentioned
- Bonding certification

## Section Structure (Recommended Order)

1. **Hero Section**
   - Headline + Subheadline
   - Primary CTA
   - Hero image/video
   - Trust bar (ratings, years in business, license #)

2. **Services Overview**
   - Icon-based service cards
   - Brief descriptions
   - Links to dedicated service pages

3. **Why Choose Us / Value Propositions**
   - 3-4 key differentiators
   - Icons with brief descriptions
   - Emergency availability highlighted

4. **Social Proof / Testimonials**
   - Customer reviews carousel
   - Star ratings from Google/Yelp
   - Before/after photos if applicable

5. **Service Areas**
   - Map or list of cities/neighborhoods served
   - Local phone numbers by area

6. **Pricing / Special Offers**
   - Current promotions
   - Financing options
   - Price transparency messaging

7. **FAQ Section**
   - 5-7 common questions
   - Address pricing, timing, and service concerns

8. **Final CTA Section**
   - Reinforced value proposition
   - Contact form
   - Phone number prominently displayed

9. **Footer**
   - Contact information
   - Service areas
   - Certifications
   - Legal links

## Objection Handling

### Common Objections & Responses

| Objection                        | How Pages Address It                                                                    |
| -------------------------------- | --------------------------------------------------------------------------------------- |
| "Is this going to be expensive?" | Upfront pricing promises, free estimates, financing options, price match guarantees     |
| "Can I trust this company?"      | Licenses displayed, insurance mentioned, BBB rating, years in business, local ownership |
| "Will they show up on time?"     | On-time guarantee, GPS-tracked technicians, appointment reminders mentioned             |
| "What if I'm not satisfied?"     | 100% satisfaction guarantee, workmanship warranty, money-back promise                   |
| "Is this an emergency?"          | 24/7 availability, same-day service, emergency response time guarantees                 |
| "Do they service my area?"       | Service area maps, local phone numbers, city-specific landing pages                     |

### FAQ Section Topics

- What does HVAC service cost?
- How long does installation take?
- Do you offer financing?
- What brands do you service?
- How quickly can you respond to emergencies?
- Are your technicians background-checked?
- What warranties do you offer?

## Conversion Blueprint

### Recommended Headline Formula

```
[Urgent Problem/Need]? Get [Specific Benefit] from [City]'s #1 [Service Type]
```

**Examples:**

- "AC Not Cooling? Get Same-Day Repair from Dallas's #1 HVAC Team"
- "Need Heating Fast? Get 24/7 Emergency Service from Austin's Most Trusted HVAC Company"

### Recommended Section Order

1. Hero (Headline, CTA, Trust Bar)
2. Services Grid (with icons)
3. Why Choose Us (3-4 value props)
4. Testimonials/Reviews
5. Special Offers/Pricing Transparency
6. FAQ Section
7. Final CTA + Contact Form
8. Footer

### Recommended Form Fields

1. Name (required)
2. Phone (required)
3. Service Needed (dropdown - required)
4. Best Time to Call (optional)

### Recommended CTA Wording

- **Primary**: "Get My Free Estimate"
- **Secondary**: "Call Now: [Phone Number]"
- **Emergency**: "Get Emergency Help Now"

### Key Conversion Triggers

1. **Localization**: Mention city/neighborhood in headline
2. **Urgency**: 24/7 availability, same-day service
3. **Trust**: License number, insurance, reviews
4. **Risk Reversal**: Free estimates, satisfaction guarantee
5. **Specificity**: Exact response time, warranty length
